The City Record dataset, at a glance — live aggregates queried straight from NYC Open Data by your browser, nothing cached server-side. Numbers here follow the honesty rules: statistics stay within their section, apparent data-entry errors (amounts ≥ $10B) are excluded, and placeholder deadlines aren't counted as real.

What the record actually contains (all time, by section)

Most of the City Record is civil-service personnel paperwork — the civic-interest notices are the minority. This is why every number on this site is per-section.
